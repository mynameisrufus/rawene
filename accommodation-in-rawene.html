Grace Cottage

Grace cottage, 37 Manning street, Rawene. A romantic cottage situated on a quiet village street, overlooking the magnificent Hokianga Harbour, central to 'must do' subtropical Northland scenery and points of interest.

Rawene is peaceful, tranquil and orchestrated by bird sounds and well away from the madding crowd.
